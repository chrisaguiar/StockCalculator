Microsoft stock rises on strong earnings report

Microsoft Corporation (MSFT) stock rose sharply on Friday after the company reported strong earnings for the fourth quarter of 2022. The software giant reported revenue of $46.2 billion, up 21% from the same period last year, and earnings per share of $2.17, beating analysts' estimates.

Apple announces new product lineup at Spring Event

Apple Inc. (AAPL) announced a range of new products at its Spring Event on Tuesday, including the latest version of its popular iPad and a new line of iMacs powered by its M2 chip. The tech giant also introduced AirTags, a new device that can be attached to items like keys and wallets to help users find them if they are lost.

Amazon announces plans to acquire MGM Studios

Amazon.com Inc. (AMZN) announced on Wednesday that it has agreed to acquire MGM Studios, the iconic film and television studio, for $8.45 billion. The deal will give Amazon access to MGM's vast library of content, which includes classic films like The Wizard of Oz and Gone with the Wind, as well as popular TV shows like The Handmaid's Tale and Fargo.
